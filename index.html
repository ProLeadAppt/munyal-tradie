How it works

Three steps. We do the work. You collect the jobs.

No call centre. No offshore scripts. No portal to log into. We connect to your phone, build the system, and run it. You keep doing what you're doing.

STEP 01 / SETUP

We connect to your phone and calendar.

We wire missed-call text-back to your number, build your voice receptionist, and connect your calendar. You keep your number. We answer the calls you're missing. Takes 30 minutes of your time.

⏱ 30 min of your time · Live in 1–2 weeks
STEP 02 / CAPTURE

We pick up. We qualify. We book.

When a call rings out after hours, on the weekend, or while you're on a job — we answer in 30 seconds. We qualify the customer (location, scope, budget window). We book the job into your calendar. You get an SMS with the customer's name, number, and what they want.

⏱ 30-second response, every time
STEP 03 / FOLLOW-THROUGH

Quotes followed up. Reviews collected.

Every quote you send gets a 24-hour, 72-hour, and 7-day follow-up. Every completed job gets a Google review request 2 hours later. You get a live dashboard showing every call, every booking, every review.

⏱ +30–50% quote close rate, typically
